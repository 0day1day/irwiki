====== FireEye appliance ======
===== Summary =====
The FireEye appliance is a device that monitors network traffic for indications of malicious traffic.  It also captures the data it sees on the wire and -if it is an executable- runs it in a VM for analysis. It also stores a copy of suspected malware for later analysis.
